Building a Korean pantry is the first step to making great gukbap at home. Here are the essential ingredients every gukbap cook should have, along with our top Amazon picks.

## The Essentials

### 1. Gochugaru (Korean Red Pepper Flakes)

고추가루 — The foundation of Korean spicy cooking. Gochugaru has a different flavor profile than other red pepper flakes — it's slightly sweet, smoky, and less intensely hot than cayenne.

| Brand | Size | Best For |
|:------|:----:|:---------|
| **Sempio** | 1 lb | All-purpose, balanced flavor |
| **Taehwa** | 12 oz | Fine grind, great for soups |
| **Chung Jung One** | 1.1 lb | Coarse grind, traditional |

**Use in gukbap:** Sprinkled on top of any gukbap for color and mild heat

### 2. Gochujang (Korean Chili Paste)

고추장 — A fermented chili paste that's sweet, spicy, and funky all at once. Essential for many Korean dishes.

| Brand | Size | Heat Level |
|:------|:----:|:----------:|
| **Sempio** | 14 oz | Medium |
| **Chung Jung One** | 17.6 oz | Mild |
| **Haetnim** | 11 oz | Hot |

**Use in gukbap:** Mixed into the broth for a spicy kick, or served on the side

### 3. Doenjang (Soybean Paste)

된장 — Korean fermented soybean paste, similar to Japanese miso but earthier and more pungent. Essential for vegetarian gukbap.

**Pick:** **Sempio Doenjang** (14 oz) — the most trusted brand in Korea

### 4. Saeujeot (Salted Fermented Shrimp)

새우젓 — Tiny salted shrimp used as a seasoning and dipping sauce. It's a critical condiment for dwaeji gukbap — the salty, funky flavor cuts through the rich pork broth perfectly.

**Pick:** **Assi Brand Saeujeot** (10 oz)

### 5. Dashima (Dried Kelp)

다시마 — Dried kelp sheets used to make vegan/vegetarian broth. Essential for kongnamul gukbap and temple-style soups.

**Pick:** **Bibigo Dashima** (1.5 oz, ~20 sheets)

### 6. Deulkkae-garu (Perilla Seed Powder)

들깨가루 — Toasted perilla seed powder, which adds a nutty, earthy flavor to sundae gukbap and seolleongtang.

**Pick:** **Jinmi Deulkkae-garu** (8 oz)

### 7. Chamgireum (Sesame Oil)

참기름 — Toasted sesame oil is used as a finishing oil for almost every gukbap. A drizzle before serving adds an incredible aroma.

**Pick:** **Ottogi Premium Sesame Oil** (3.4 oz)

## Budget-Friendly Tips

- **Buy gochugaru in bulk** — It keeps for months in the freezer
- **Dashima lasts forever** — A single pack can make 50+ batches of broth
- **Saeujeot goes a long way** — A little jar lasts for months
- **Doenjang is versatile** — Use it for soups, sauces, and marinades beyond gukbap
