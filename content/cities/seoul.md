Seoul is the epicenter of Korean food culture, and gukbap is no exception. With thousands of restaurants across the city's 25 districts, finding the best bowls requires some guidance. Here's our district-by-district breakdown.

## Jongno-gu — The Historic Heart

Jongno is home to Seoul's oldest restaurants, including legendary gukbap spots that have been operating for over a century.

### Must-Visit Spots

| Restaurant | Type | Since | Address Area |
|------------|------|:-----:|--------------|
| **Imun Seolnongtang** | Seolleongtang | 1904 | Jongno 4-ga |
| **Hadongkwan** | Beef gukbap | 1939 | Myeong-dong |
| **Woo Lae Oak** | Galbi-tang | 1946 | Euljiro |

## Jung-gu — Myeong-dong & Around

Myeong-dong is tourist central, but there are gems hiding in the side streets.

- **Hadongkwan** — The legendary beef soup restaurant that's been operating since 1939. Their *gukbap* (beef soup with rice) is the gold standard.
- **Myeong-dong Kyoja** — Famous for *kalguksu*, but their gukbap is solid too.

## Mapo-gu — Hongdae & Beyond

The younger crowd favors Mapo for its more modern, experimental takes on traditional dishes.

- **Gangchon Gukbap** — Known for a spicy, modern take on dwaeji gukbap
- **Mapo Gukbap** — A no-frills local joint with excellent value

## Dongdaemun-gu

Open 24 hours, Dongdaemun is perfect for late-night gukbap cravings.

- **Jangchungdong Sora** — Famous sundae gukbap
- **Pildong Myeonok** — Traditional *naengmyeon* and gukbap

## Yongsan-gu

Home to Itaewon, Yongsan offers some international fusion takes on gukbap alongside traditional versions.

## Pro Tips for Seoul

1. **Go early for lunch** — Most gukbap restaurants serve from 10 AM, and popular spots run out of some menu items by 1 PM
2. **Look for "이용" signs** — "이용" in Korean restaurant names often indicates a long-established restaurant
3. **Don't skip the sides** — The quality of kimchi and *kkakdugi* is a tell for overall restaurant quality
4. **Ask for "sigeumchi-namul"** — Many seolleongtang places offer seasoned spinach as a complimentary side
5. **Use KakaoMap or Naver Map** — Google Maps doesn't work well for navigation in Seoul
