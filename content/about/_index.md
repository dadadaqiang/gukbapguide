**Gukbap Guide** is the English internet's first dedicated resource for everything about gukbap — Korea's beloved soup rice dish.

## Our Mission

We believe gukbap deserves a place alongside ramen, pho, and bibimbap in the global culinary conversation. Our goal is to:

- **Educate** — Explain what gukbap is and why it matters
- **Guide** — Help you find the best bowls wherever you are
- **Inspire** — Share recipes so you can make it at home
- **Connect** — Build a community around Korean food culture

## Why Gukbap?

Gukbap is having a moment. Featured by the NYT Cooking, named a top trend by Korea's largest food platform Baemin, and spotlighted on Netflix — Korean soup rice is finally getting the global attention it deserves.
